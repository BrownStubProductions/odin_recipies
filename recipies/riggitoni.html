Riggitoni

A delicous tubular pasta, served with fresh bolognese sauce. Includes shrimp, parsley and peppercorns.

  • Pasta
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Parsley
  • Tomato Paste
  • Peppercorns
  1. Put Bolognese in a hot pan and heat
  2. Add Tomato paste and peppercorns
  3. Add pasta to boiling water
  4. Cook until el dente
  5. Mix pasta with sauce and parsley
