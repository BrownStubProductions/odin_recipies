Linguini

A stringy pasta, made in alfredo sauce. Sprinkled in herbs, pepper and parmesean cheese.

  • Linguini
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Herbs
  • Parmesean
  • Butter
  1. Heat up Alfredo in a hot pan
  2. Cook pasta in boiling water
  3. Add some pasta water to a new hot pan.
  4. Cook until el dente
  5. Mix pasta in pan with sauce and pasta water
  6. add the herbs
  7. grate parm cheese over top to serve
